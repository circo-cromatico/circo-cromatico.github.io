"In that unknown land, anything was possible. Neither the landscape, nor the people, nor the animals would be like anything seen anywhere else on Earth.[..]

[..]Over the course of time, Patagonia stopped being a complete mystery. First Magellan and the a veritable legion of seamen, explorers, adventurers, pirates and scientists would make it the business to travel it and discover it and to make what until then had been a secret, into part of they "known world". Despite this kind of interest, the region never completely lost its original nature. And even today, when the differences are being extinguished and surprises are ever fewer, Patagonia continues to exert an irresistible attraction over all those who feel the need to know what starts where the road ends"..
